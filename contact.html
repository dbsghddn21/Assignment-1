Message to customer

Welcome to Gund-arm Co, the Home of Gundam in Canada!

Welcome to our world! At Gund-arm Co, we're more than just an e-store; we're a community of passionate model builders and Gundam fans spread across Canada. Our mission is to bring you the widest selection of Gundam model kits, from the classic to the latest releases, ensuring that every builder, from beginners to seasoned veterans, finds their next project and a source of endless inspiration right here.

Your Experience, Our Commitment: We understand the joy and satisfaction that comes from assembling a Gundam model, which is why we are committed to providing not only the best products but also expert advice, tutorials, and customer service to enhance your building experience. We're here to support you at every step of your model-building journey, from selection to assembly, and beyond.

Building Together: Our vision goes beyond selling model kits. We aim to foster a vibrant community where builders can share their experiences, tips, and creations. Whether through our forums, social media, or customer reviews, your stories and feedback are what shape us. We believe that together, we can create an inclusive space that celebrates creativity, skill development, and the shared love for Gundam.

Your Voice Matters: Your satisfaction is our top priority. If you have suggestions, questions, or just want to share your latest build with us, don't hesitate to reach out. We're here to listen and continuously improve based on your feedback.

Thank you for choosing Gund-arm Co. We're excited to be part of your model-building journey and can't wait to see what you create!
