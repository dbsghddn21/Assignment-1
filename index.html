About us

Mission

Our mission is to empower Canadian Gundam enthusiasts by providing an unparalleled selection of Gundam model kits, accessories, and resources. We are dedicated to fostering a vibrant community of model builders, offering expert advice, and delivering exceptional customer service. By making the latest and most sought-after models accessible, we aim to inspire creativity, skill development, and a deep appreciation for the art of model building.

Vision

Our vision is to become Canada's premier destination for Gundam model builders, recognized for our extensive range, community engagement, and contribution to the growth of the hobby. We envision a future where every Canadian model builder, regardless of skill level, finds the support, resources, and products they need to achieve their creative potential. Through innovation, collaboration, and an unwavering commitment to our customers, we aim to cultivate a culture that celebrates the artistry and camaraderie of Gundam model building.
